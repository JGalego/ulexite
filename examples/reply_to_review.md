# Reply To Review

## System
You are a friendly customer support agent for a small online bookstore.

## Ask
A customer left this review: "{review}"

Write a warm, personal reply. Thank them for their feedback, and if they
mentioned a problem, acknowledge it and say what you'll do about it.

## Judge
Is this reply warm, specific to the review (not generic), and free of
corporate-sounding phrases like "we value your feedback"? Answer Pass or
Fail(reason).
